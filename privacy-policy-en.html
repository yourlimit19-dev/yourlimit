Privacy Policy - YourLimit

Privacy Policy

App: YourLimit Last Updated: July 7, 2026 Contact: yourlimit19@gmail.com

1. Introduction

YourLimit (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a personal finance management application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. By using the app, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register in the app, we collect: - Email address - Other account identification information (e.g., name, user ID)

2.2 Financial Information

YourLimit’s core function is managing personal income and expenses. For this purpose, you input the following information yourself: - Monthly income amount - Expense categories and amounts - Budget and financial goals - Other finance-related notes

Important note: This financial data is stored securely on our servers and/or your device, and is used solely to generate analysis and reports for you. This data is never sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.

2.3 Usage Analytics

To improve app performance, we collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most, crash occurrences).

2.5 Advertising Data

Our app uses Google AdMob to display ads to free-tier users. For this purpose, AdMob may collect data such as your device’s Advertising ID, approximate location (country/city level), and ad interaction data (impressions, clicks). This data is used to personalize ads and measure ad performance.

For Premium subscribers: No ads are shown to users with an active Premium subscription, and none of the advertising data described in this section is collected for them.

You can review Google AdMob’s privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and Switzerland: When you first open the app, you will be shown a consent prompt asking for your preferences regarding ad personalization. You can change your choice at any time from the app’s Settings section.

2.4 Automatically Collected Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Service Purpose
Firebase (Google) Crash reporting and anonymous usage analytics
RevenueCat Subscription and payment management
Google AdMob Displaying ads to free-tier users (see section 2.5)

These services have their own privacy policies: - Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy - RevenueCat: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy - Google AdMob: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Advertising: The free version of our app displays ads via Google AdMob (see section 2.5). Premium subscribers are shown no ads at all. Advertising data is never sold to third parties beyond AdMob.

4. Subscription and Payment Information

YourLimit operates on a subscription basis. Payment transactions are processed through Google Play Billing and managed by RevenueCat. We do not directly store your credit card or bank information — this data is held securely by Google Play and RevenueCat.

5. Purpose of Data Use

We use the information we collect to: - Provide the app’s core functionality (income/expense analysis) - Create and manage your account - Track subscription status - Detect and resolve technical issues - Improve the app

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on secure servers using encryption.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for the app to function.
  • Appropriate technical measures are in place to protect your data from unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: - Access: View the information we hold about you - Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information - Deletion: Delete your account and associated data - Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at yourlimit19@gmail.com.

8. Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your account: - You can delete it directly from the app’s “Settings” section, OR - You can email us at yourlimit19@gmail.com with a deletion request

Once your account is deleted, your personal information (email, financial records) will be removed from our systems, except where retention is required by law.

9. Children’s Privacy

YourLimit is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover such data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through an in-app notice. The most current version of this policy will always be available on this page.
