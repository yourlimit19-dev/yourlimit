YourLimit — Account Deletion

In-app (recommended)

  1. Open the YourLimit app
  2. Go to the Profile section
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion (you may be asked to re-enter your password)

Data that will be deleted

  • Name and email address
  • All financial records (expenses, income, accounts)
  • Goals
  • Premium subscription status

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Can't access the app?
Email support@yourlimit.com with the subject "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your account. Your request will be processed within 30 days.
